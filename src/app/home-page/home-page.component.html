Gambero Rosso - that means feeling good and enjoying the special Italian atmosphere. To be pampered by the Gambero Rosso team and to dine by romantic candlelight. Homemade pasta

Homemade pasta and wines from the best Italian growing regions. Gambero Rosso is your Italian retreat in Vienna's city centre - 2 minutes from Stephansplatz and the U3 station Stubentor. Open daily, continuous warm cuisine Monday- Sunday from 11:30 to 22:00.
